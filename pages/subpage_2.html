    • Careers in Web development using UI and UX are very diverse

    Web development is one of the most dynamic and in-demand fields in the tech industry. It involves creating and maintaining websites or web applications that are accessible via the internet. Jobs in this field are abundant and diverse, ranging from front-end development to back-end development, full-stack roles, and even specialized positions in areas like web design, UX/UI, and mobile web development.
